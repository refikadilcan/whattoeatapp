# What to Eat: Dice or Duel

Can't decide what to eat? This app decides for you.

**What to Eat: Dice or Duel** is a simple iOS app that helps you pick your next meal when you're stuck in the endless "what should I eat?" loop.

## Features

### Dice Mode
Tap the button and let fate decide. The app randomly picks a meal from a curated list of popular foods — burger, pizza, kebab, sushi, ramen, and more.

### Duel Mode
Two foods go head-to-head in a tournament-style bracket. Pick your favorite in each round until one winner remains.

### Find Nearby
Once you've decided what to eat, tap "Find Nearby" to locate restaurants on Apple Maps or Google Maps.

## Food Options

Burger, Pizza, Kebab, Sushi, Italian Pasta, Taco, Steak, Falafel, Noodle/Ramen, Sandwich, Fried Chicken

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0+
- Xcode 16.0+
