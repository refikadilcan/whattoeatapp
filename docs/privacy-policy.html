Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

What to Eat: Dice or Duel ("the App") is developed by Refik Adilcan Egilmez.

Information We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require account registration or login.

Location Data

When you tap "Find Nearby", the app requests your current location to provide more accurate restaurant results on the map. Your location is used only at that moment and is never stored, collected, or shared with any third party. The app works normally even if you decline the location permission.

Data Usage

The App functions entirely on your device. No personal data is sent to or stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. When you use the "Find Nearby" feature, the app opens Apple Maps or Google Maps with your search query.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
