FAQ

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
A: No. The app works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed if you tap "Find Nearby" to open a map.

Q: Is the app free?
A: Yes, the app is free to use.

Q: Which devices are supported?
A: The app supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.6 or later.

Q: Why does the app ask for my location?
A: When you tap "Find Nearby", the app uses your current location to show more accurate restaurant results on the map. Your location is never stored or shared — it's only used at that moment to improve search results. The app works fine even if you decline the permission.
